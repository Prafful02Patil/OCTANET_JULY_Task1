Welcome to SVKM's NMIMS!

Experience the best of education and industry-oriented programs at our picturesque riverside campus. Our carefully chosen faculty members and regular interactions with industry experts ensure that you are well-equipped to meet the demands of the industry. We offer programs in Engineering, Pharmacy, Agriculture and Textile, providing a multi-disciplinary education to all our students. Our residential campus fosters a holistic learning environment, encouraging all-round development and self-directed learning. Join us today and be a part of our alumni community of achievers and leaders.
